Introduction

Robotics is a multidisciplinary field that involves the design, construction, operation, and use of robots to perform tasks in various industries. It combines aspects of mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, computer science, and artificial intelligence. Robotics has applications in manufacturing, healthcare, entertainment, and more
